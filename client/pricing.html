Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from the Billing page. Your plan stays active until the end of your billing period.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

All your receipts remain stored on your device in IndexedDB. You'll simply return to the 200-receipt limit for new entries.

Is my card information stored?

Never. All payments are handled securely by Stripe. ReceiptVault AI never sees or stores your card details.

Is there a trial period?

The Free plan is unlimited in time. You can use it forever and upgrade whenever you need more.
